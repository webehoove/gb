02. Colors

The Diligence in Gaming color palette is one of the brand’s most powerful and recognisable assets.

Our most iconic color is the DiG Blue. White and grey provide the backdrop for much of the brand, and in combination with the accent DiG Hepatica, which builds on Diligence in Gaming’ established brand equity, pairs to create a dynamic and recognisable combination.

Primary colors

We use GB Moss, GB Cashew and GB White as our key signifier. It creates a distinct and recognisable sender. These colors are most commonly used for the logotype, but can also be applied to create impact and dynamic expression typographically.

03. Typography

  • Arabic Headline
  • Arabic Body copy
  • Latin Headline
  • Latin Body copy

Typography is an essential component of the visual identity. Brand typefaces give us a new voice that’s both friendly and authoritative, while ensuring a uniformity across all touchpoints and forms of communication.

The identity places a heavy emphasis on contemporary typographic expression, but also ensures clarity of communication doesn’t suffer as a result. Brand typefaces, and the treatment of it, creates a visual coherence across all of our communication and helps us increase recognition and strengthen our brand.

  • El Messiri on Google Fonts
  • Cairo on Google Fonts

Our Brand Typefaces

Diligence in Gaming brand using Neue Haas Grotesk Display typeface's weights each have their own functionality and uses, meaning that headlines, body copy, and supporting typographic detail are all accounted for. With these options available, it’s easy to communicate clearly across all types of application, from larger messaging to smaller, more informative texts.

The evident contrast between the weights ensures there’s a clear definition between them, and means it’s easy to achieve typographic hierarchy whenever necessary.
